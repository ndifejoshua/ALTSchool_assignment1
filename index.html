Biography

Joshua Chukwuebuka Ndife is a passionate and goal-driven individual with a background in Material and Metallurgical Engineering, he combines problem-solving skills from his engineering training with a strong enthusiasm for technology and software development.

Joshua graduated as the Best Student in his department with a CGPA of 4.83, earning the Best Graduating Student Award. His research experience includes projects on lithium mineral beneficiation, aluminum matrix composites, and the synthesis of hydroxyapatite from eggshells.

His journey into Frontend Engineering was driven by a deep curiosity about how technology shapes user experiences and how intuitive, efficient, and visually appealing interfaces can enhance digital interactions. Joshua is self-taught in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, continuously expanding his knowledge to build responsive and interactive web applications.

Beyond software development, Joshua is a Graduate Member of the Nigerian Society of Engineers (NSE), Nigerian Institution of Mechanical Engineers (NIMechE), and the Materials Science and Technology Society of Nigeria (MSN). He is also passionate about mentorship, community development, and environmental advocacy, volunteering for initiatives like Plogging Nigeria, YALI Network. .

Why I Joined AltSchool Africa

My decision to join AltSchool Africa was driven by my desire to transition into software development, particularly Frontend Engineering. While i considered the self-taught experience, I realized that a well-structured learning, mentorship, and hands-on approach(project-based curriculum) would accelerate my learning and help me become an industry-ready developer.

Additionally, my personal experience during a family trip where I struggled to find medical help due to language barrier and lack of digital health solutions, strengthened my resolve to use technology for impactful change

AltSchool stood out to me because of its practical learning model, industry-aligned curriculum, experienced instructors and collaborative student community. The opportunity to work on real-world projects, receive mentorship, and develop industry-relevant skills made this program the perfect fit for my growth and learning goals.

Financial constraint also played a role in my decision, making the Ayoba Developer Program Scholarship a critical support system in my journey towards becoming a skilled software developer
